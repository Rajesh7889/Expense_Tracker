Things you should know about this application..

  • No more need of notebooks and pens to maintain your Expense-records ..
  • You can create multiple books to maitain and track expence records of multiple fields..
  • Each book will maintain different record with date and time of updation which you can edit any timme..
  • Each record will maintained properly and your data will be secure from other users..

How to use?

  • To use this application , you have to register first..
  • Login with same email and password using which you are registered yourself..
  • After login , you will be able to use our application..

Functionalities..

  • click-on create-book , give it an identifier , and click on create.(you can create any number of books).
  • click on the book you created.. your book will be opened..
  • click on cashin\cashout as per requirement..your whole record will be maintained will date and time of adding entries..
  • click on edit icon to edit the specific record and then click on the tick icon to update...
  • click on delete to delete an entery..
